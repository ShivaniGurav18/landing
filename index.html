DESTINATION

Rajasthan, the land of kings, is a vibrant state in India known for its rich cultural heritage, majestic forts, and stunning palaces. Popular destinations include Jaipur, the Pink City, known for the Hawa Mahal and Amer Fort; Udaipur, the City of Lakes, with its romantic Lake Palace; and Jaisalmer, the Golden City, famed for its desert safaris and the magnificent Jaisalmer Fort.

PINK CITY

Jaipur, the Pink City, captivates with its blend of historical and cultural treasures. Key attractions include the majestic Amer Fort, the intricate Hawa Mahal, and the opulent City Palace.

UDAIPUR

Udaipur, the City of Lakes, enchants with its serene waters, regal palaces, and lush gardens. Key attractions include the stunning Lake Palace and the tranquil Fateh Sagar Lake.

JAISALMER

Jaisalmer, the Golden City, is famed for its sandstone architecture. Key attractions include the magnificent Jaisalmer Fort and the serene Gadisar Lake.
